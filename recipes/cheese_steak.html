Cheese Steaks

Description

Philly Cheesesteak with tender ribeye steak, melted gooey provolone, and caramelized onions hugged by a toasted garlic butter hoagie roll. This is the classic way to make a Philly Cheesesteak sandwich!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 lb Ribeye Steak (thinly sliced)
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Pinch of Pepper
  • 1/4 cup Sweet Onions
  • 1 Hoagie Roll
  • 3 Slice of Butter
  • Spoonful of Mayonnaise or to taste

Directions

  1. Slice hoagie rolls 3/4 of the way through with a serrated knife. Dice onions and thinly slice beef.
  2. Soften firsst slice of butter and spread onto hoagie roll. Toast roll on a large skillet on medium heat until golden brown then set aside.
  3. Use second slice fo butter to coat pan and sautee diced onions until caramelized then transfer to a bowl.
  4. Increase to high heat and use last slice of butter to recoat the pan. Spread the thinly sliced steak into a flat layer in the pan and let brown for a couple of minutes then flip and season with salt and pepper
  5. Once steak is cooked to prefered temperature place remove from heat. Spread a layer of mayonnaise on hoagie roll then place steak onto the roll
  6. Add any other toppings you desire
  7. Ravenously enjoy this delicously, masterful piece of art you just created
