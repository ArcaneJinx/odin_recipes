Grilled Cheese

Description

gooey, strecthy, cheesey piece of heaven betwixt two buttery, golden crusted pieces of white bread

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of White bread
  • 2 Slice of Butter
  • 2 Slices of Muenster Cheese

Directions

  1. Heat skillet on low heat and add the slice of butter.
  2. Assembly sandwich with two pieces of cheese between two pieces of bread and put into skillet
  3. Wait until bottom side of the grilled cheese is golden brown (about 5-7 minutes) then add second slice of butter and flip the sandwhich.
  4. Wait until bottom side is golden brown (about 3-5 minutes> then diagonally slice sandwhich and plate.
  5. Enjoy The Best grilld cheese you'll ever have!
